About Our Program

Municipal Park Raiders Cheer is a youth cheer program serving athletes ages 4–12 in Mobile, Alabama. Our program focuses on teamwork, confidence, leadership, and community pride. Each team has dedicated coaches and a dance coach who helps prepare athletes for cheer competitions in November.
